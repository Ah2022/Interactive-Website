## Notes:
- About section uses split layout with image/avatar on left, bio on right

- Interactive timeline shows career progression with expandable details
- Personal interests section uses grid of interactive cards
- Micro-interactions throughout page on scroll and hover
- Background gradient shifts subtly based on scroll position
